
Install this dependency: 
Download from: https://github.com/trulia/trulia-java-rets-client 
	Note: You must download the zip and extract the jar-with-dependencies
mvn install:install-file -Dfile=./trulia-java-rets-client-local-jar-with-dependencies.jar -DgroupId=org.realtors.rets.client -DartifactId=trulia-rets-client-with-dependencies -Dversion=1.0 -Dpackaging=jar
